Executive Summary

Project Overview

This proposal outlines a comprehensive digital transformation for Hotel Udhayam International in three phases:

  • Phase 1: Build complete hotel management software from scratch (Front Desk, Restaurant, Billing, Expenses, Receipts, Inventory, Staff Management)
  • Phase 2: Digitize management reports (A, B, J formats) with instant WhatsApp sharing to management
  • Phase 3: Implement PIR sensors for real-time room occupancy detection and revenue leakage prevention

Phase 3: PIR Sensor Integration

3
Real-Time Room Occupancy Detection

Implement PIR sensors in all rooms to detect unauthorized occupancy and prevent revenue leakage through real-time monitoring.

Revenue Leakage Points

!
Problem: Hotels typically lose 5-15% of revenue through unauthorized transactions that go undetected.
Common Leakage Scenarios
Unauthorized Check-ins

Staff allows short stays, collects cash, no record in system

Pocket Collection

Payment collected from guest but not entered in system

Restaurant Fraud

Orders served but bill not generated, cash pocketed

Inventory Theft

Stock consumed but not accounted in sales

Our Solution

Control What It Catches How It Works
PIR Sensors Unauthorized room occupancy Detects motion in "Available" rooms
Photo Capture Fake bookings Guest ID & face required at check-in
Advance Enforcement Walk-away guests Minimum payment required, override needs PIN
KOT Tracking Unbilled orders Every KOT must have bill or authorized cancel
Stock Reconciliation Inventory theft Match consumption with sales
Activity Logs Staff misbehavior All actions logged with timestamp
